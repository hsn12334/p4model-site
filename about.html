About the P4 Model

Our mission is to bring hope and progress to underserved communities through the P4 strategy. We aim to ensure its core message resonates deeply across every corner of society. The goal is to bridge the divide between the affluent and the underprivileged. The government has set a target to identify 15 lakh households, known as 'Bangaru Kutumbams' or golden families, and appoint 1,00,000 mentors by August 15. These mentors called Margadarsis will offer initial support and continued guidance. Support must go beyond a one-time donation; it requires consistent engagement. These Margadarsis can be individuals from industries, philanthropists, NRIs, or anyone already aiding the needy. They will be brought onto the P4 platform to serve with compassion and commitment. With P4, our shared vision is to eradicate poverty from Andhra Pradesh by 2029. It is an ambitious mission one that demands dedication, but with united will, we believe it's within reach. The top 10% of wealth creators are encouraged to uplift the bottom 20%, nurturing a culture of purposeful giving.

Types of Support

  • Educational funding, healthcare assistance, and business support
  • Career and financial mentoring
  • Access to professional circles and opportunities
  • Tuition sponsorships or micro-investment for small businesses
  • Community-focused infrastructure projects

Government’s Responsibility

  • Identify Bangaru Kutumbams using analytical tools and local surveys
  • Ensure families continue to benefit from current welfare schemes
  • Oversee policy development and scheme monitoring—not direct financial handling

Role of Margadarsis (Mentors)

  • Choose families to support through the P4 digital platform
  • Collaborate through government-facilitated introductions and communication
  • Offer mentorship, funding, educational and entrepreneurial aid
  • Use the platform to ensure transparency and track impact metrics

Financial Flow and Accountability

  • Beneficiaries or local leaders list financial requirements on the P4 portal
  • Requests are validated by certified public institutions
  • All financial support is routed through the platform for clear and accountable delivery
